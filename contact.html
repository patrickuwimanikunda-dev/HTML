Quick answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take? +
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express (1–2 days) and same-day options are available for orders over $99 in eligible areas.
Are all products genuine? +
Absolutely. Every item is sourced directly from manufacturers or authorized distributors and passes our authenticity verification process before listing.
What's your return policy? +
We offer hassle-free 30-day returns on all items. Simply initiate a return from your account dashboard and drop the package at any partner location.
Do you offer warranty on products? +
All products come with the manufacturer's standard warranty. Our optional Pateq Care plan extends coverage up to 3 years with priority support.
Can I track my order? +
Yes! Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. Real-time tracking is also available from your Pateq account.
Do you ship internationally? +
We ship to 48+ countries. International delivery typically takes 5–10 business days. Customs fees may apply based on your country's regulations.
