Who we are

Tech, Simplified.
Performance, Amplified.

Founded in 2018 in the heart of Silicon Valley, Pateq started as a small team of engineers and design enthusiasts who were frustrated with the state of electronics retail — too much noise, too little substance.

We set out to change that. Today, Pateq is a global destination for discerning tech buyers who value precision, honesty, and genuine expertise over flashy marketing.

"We don't just sell devices. We connect people to the tools that expand what's possible in their lives."
— Kaito Patel, Founder & CEO

From flagship smartphones to professional-grade audio, every product on Pateq is hand-selected, authenticity-verified, and backed by our world-class support team.

Our journey

How We Got Here

2018
Founded in San Francisco
Kaito Patel and three co-founders launch Pateq as an online-only electronics retailer, focusing on quality over quantity with a curated catalog of 200 products.
2019
First Flagship Store Opens
Our first physical store opens in SF's SoMa district, featuring an immersive demo space where customers can try before they buy.
2021
International Expansion
Pateq expands to Europe and Asia-Pacific, opening distribution hubs in London, Tokyo, and Singapore to serve global demand.
2023
Pateq Care Program Launches
We introduce our industry-leading extended warranty and premium support program, covering every product we sell for up to 3 years.
2025
2 Million Customers Milestone
We hit 2 million satisfied customers worldwide and launch our redesigned platform — faster, smarter, and more personalized than ever.
